Follow Super Easy Steps

Start bidding for the lowest-priced vehicles in S.Korea.

1.Register for Free

To check the live auctions, first step is to Sign up for Auctionwini Basic membership to find your vehicles with the lowest price possible. 37,000+ buyers from 180+ countries are already browsing our inventories in live auctions for free!

2.Upgrade to Premium

Want to bid without limits?
Upgrade to Premium membership with your government-issued photo ID and 100% refundable, one-time $300 deposit.

No other fees or requirements!

    • Request on-demand shipping options

  • Basic

03.Bid on your vehicle

Live auctions are happening every week!
Find your vehicles, save them in your watchlist, and be notified before auction begins.

Can't join the auction on time?
Don't worry. We have pre-bid system which allows you to bid on vehicles until 15-min before the auction begins.

04.Confirm and Pay

Are you the highest bidder on the vehicle?
Confirm your order and pay the total amount. Once the full amount has been paid 100% through bank transfer, we schedule your vehicle for delivery.

Exclusive Auctionwini Services
05.Wait for your delivery

Our logistics team have shipped about 30,000 vehicles per year all around the world. Just fill in your details for shipping, we will book on the fastest vessel and update your ‘My Order’ page to track the order yourself.

Frequently Asked Questions
  • Why can't I place a bid?

    Please check your membership status, only premium members can bid in a live auction. If you complete all steps(ID upload & Place deposit) for your membership, wait 1-3 business days to be processed. we will contact you as soon as we confirm your membership. Check membership status

  • Can I cancel my bid?

    Once a bid is placed, it cannot be canceled or retracted. Make sure that you are fully confident in your bid before you place it.

  • What is included in the Final Price?

    Once you win a lot(vehicle), you will receive an invoice with,

    1. Lot Price* (price at which the lot was sold during the auction)
    2. Carrier Charge*
    3. Auction Charge*
    4. Shipping Cost (depends on your trade conditions)

    If you need more information about these charges, you can calculate your estimated total amount by using the calculator. Check Fees & Costs

  • How can I pay?

    100% full payment amount must be paid via bank transfer. We do not accept partial payment, credit card, or paypal.

    * Deposit can be paid via Paypal.
